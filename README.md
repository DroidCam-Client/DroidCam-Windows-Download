## 🧠 Overview

The software establishes a connection between your phone and PC through either a wireless network or a USB cable. Once linked, the device functions as a standard webcam input, making it compatible with a wide range of video conferencing and streaming platforms.

Its design focuses on simplicity, enabling users to get started quickly without complicated configuration. At the same time, it offers useful customization options such as resolution adjustments and frame rate control to match different performance needs.

DroidCam is particularly useful for remote work, online communication, and content creation, where video clarity plays an important role. It also serves as a practical solution for extending the usefulness of older smartphones.

With its balance of ease of use and functionality, **DroidCam** provides an efficient way to enhance your video setup using devices you already own.
